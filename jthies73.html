// Philosophy

The Problem Solver Paradigm

I’ve come to view software engineering as the art of managing complexity. My experience across enterprise finance, healthcare, and various side projects has taught me that the stack matters less than the strategy. True challenge lies in the discipline of decoupling complexity. Breaking down the overwhelming into the manageable. While the industry rewards specificity in tools, I believe those tools are transient. I focus instead on the enduring logic of the system, ensuring the structural foundation remains resilient regardless of how the technology evolves.
